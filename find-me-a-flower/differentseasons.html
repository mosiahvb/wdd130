Different Seasons

Fun fact

When you go to a flower shop or a grocery store often times they give you a little packet of plant food. A lot of people will buy more plant food just to keep their plants alive and looking beautiful longer. The plant food is actually just sugar and water, And if you mix a little sugar in your flowers water at home, they will stay looking beautiful for far longer!

Spring & Summer

Seasons will dictate the supply and demand of the variety of flowers available throughout the year. It is important to know what is in season and where these flowers are coming from. For example and the first of the spring flowers are tulips, daffodils, and hyacinth. Tulips originate from central Asia, while daffodils come from Spain and Portugal. In the summer we get roses and carnations some of the most popular flowers. An interesting fact, the majority of straight stem roses come from Ecuador.

Fall & Winter

During the fall we get beautiful flowers such as, Dahlias, and Cymbidium Orchids. The Cymbidium Orchids originate from Asia and parts of Australia. During the winter season some of the most popular flowers include Casablanca Lilies, and Forget-me-nots. The Casablanca Lilies can be found all throughout Europe. Now as you have read, there are many different beautiful options for each season! So rest assured, regardless of the season, there are beautiful flowers to fit your needs!
